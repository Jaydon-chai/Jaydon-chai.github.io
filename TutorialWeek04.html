Q6. Describe how to quantify muscle and joint function afterreverse total shoulder arthroplasty.

  • Using measurements and modelling for
    • Muscle moment arms (muscle leverage)
      • Measure the perpendicular distance between a muscle’s line ofaction and the joint centre of rotation
    • Muscle line of action
      • Vector of the force produced by a muscle
      • Estimates muscle stabilising or destabilising potential
    • Muscle forces
      • Cannot be easily measured, but can use patient specific models
      • Compare muscle force at different joint positions
    • Joint forces
      • Again requires patient specific models
